Training and Placement Cell

Bridging Talent with Opportunity

The Training and Placement Cell is a pivotal component of our institution, dedicated to providing students with the skills and opportunities necessary to build successful careers. We serve as the vital link between our talented students and the leading corporations of the industry.

About Placement Cell

Our Placement Cell operates with a clear vision: to empower our students to become competent and sought-after professionals. Through a comprehensive approach, we organize workshops, seminars, and training sessions on a regular basis. Our programs focus on enhancing communication, leadership, and technical skills, ensuring that every student is well-prepared to meet the demands of the corporate world. We maintain strong ties with a diverse range of companies, from startups to multinational corporations, to provide a wide array of placement opportunities.

Placements

Our Placement Process

The placement process is streamlined to be efficient and transparent. It typically involves a pre-placement talk, written tests, group discussions, and personal interviews. Our team works closely with students to prepare them for each stage, offering mock interview sessions and resume-building workshops.

Recruiting Partners

We are proud of our extensive network of recruiting partners. Leading companies from various sectors including IT, core engineering, finance, and marketing visit our campus every year to recruit our students. Our consistent track record of high placement rates speaks volumes about the quality of our students and our placement programs.

Industry Readiness Program

Our unique Industry Readiness Program is designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry expectations. The program includes:

  • Technical Workshops on cutting-edge technologies
  • Mock Interviews and Group Discussion practice
  • Resume and Cover Letter Writing Assistance
  • Corporate Etiquette and Professionalism training
